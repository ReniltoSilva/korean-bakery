About us

We started this bakery with one simple dream: to bring the warm, comforting flavors of Korea to Hanoi. Every bun, cake, and pastry is made from scratch using traditional Korean recipes passed down through generations — with a little love added at every step.


Our ingredients are sourced fresh daily, and everything is baked in-house so you always get the softest, most flavorful treat possible. Whether you're a longtime fan of Korean food or trying it for the first time. Our ingredients are sourced fresh daily, and everything is baked in-house.
